Frequently Asked Questions

What's the advantage of Excel over CSV?

Excel supports multiple sheets, formulas, charts, formatting, pivot tables, and data validation - features not available in plain CSV files.

Will my data be preserved exactly?

Yes! All data including numbers, text, dates, and special characters are preserved accurately during conversion.

Can I open the Excel file in Google Sheets?

Absolutely! The XLSX format is compatible with Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, LibreOffice Calc, and other spreadsheet applications.

What if my CSV uses semicolons instead of commas?

Our converter automatically detects different delimiters including semicolons, tabs, and pipes.
