Convert PNG to WebP - Smaller Size, Same Quality

Convert your PNG images to the modern WebP format. WebP offers superior compression for images on the web, drastically reducing file sizes while maintaining transparency and quality.

How to Convert PNG to WebP?

  1. Upload Files: Drag and drop PNG images or browse.
  2. Batch Convert: Processing multiple images at once is supported.
  3. Convert: Click 'Convert to WebP' to start.
  4. Download: Save WebP files individually or as a ZIP archive.

Why use WebP?

  • Smaller File Sizes: WebP images are 26% smaller than PNGs.
  • Faster Loading: Smaller images mean faster website load times.
  • Transparency Support: Unlike JPG, WebP supports transparent backgrounds.
  • High Quality: Keep your image details sharp.
